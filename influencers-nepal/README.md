## About Influencers Nepal

Influencers Nepal is a platform designed to solve the disconnect between creators and businesses in Nepal. Using smart algorithms, it pairs creative talent with brands that genuinely align with their audience.

### Key Features
- **Smart Matching**: Automated pairing of creators and brands.
- **Authentic Growth**: Focus on genuine engagement and real results.
- **Sustainable Earnings**: Helping creators build lasting income streams.
- **Built-in Communication**: Direct chat features for seamless collaboration.
- **Performance Tracking**: Detailed analytics for both influencers and businesses.

## How to Get Involved

### For Influencers
1. **Sign Up**: Create your profile on the platform.
2. **Link Accounts**: Authenticate your social media for data integration.
3. **Get Matched**: Connect with brands that fit your niche.

### For Businesses
1. **Sign Up**: Register as a business entity.
2. **Find Influencers**: Use the platform to discover the right creators for your campaigns.
3. **Collaborate & Track**: Communicate directly and monitor performance.

## How InfluencersNepal Works? (Testing Phase)

1. **Sign Up**
   Influencers fill out our Google Form with social media details and a minimum quote (starting at **Rs. 500**).

2. **Verified Listings**
   Our n8n automation validates your profile and lists it in our centralized directory.

3. **Get Discovered**
   Local businesses browse the directory to find creators that align with their niche.

4. **Direct Negotiation**
   Businesses contact influencers directly via provided communication channels. All negotiations and payments happen externally.

5. **Future Platform**
   We're building a full-fledged marketplace to facilitate secure payments and campaign tracking, launching after our **1000th influencer** joins.

## About Our Mission

We built this platform to solve a disconnect: Nepal's creators drown in self-promotion while businesses struggle to find verified, niche-specific influencers. 

During this **market testing phase**, we are a zero-commission directory. Our goal is to reach **1000 active influencers** to validate the demand for a full-scale marketplace that will eventually handle built-in chat, payments, and performance analytics.

### Current Features
- **Centralized Directory**: Search by niche, platform, and reach.
- **n8n Automation**: Real-time profile updates and validation.
- **Direct Access**: Contact influencers directly without intermediaries.
- **Low Entry Barrier**: Engagement starting from just **Rs. 500**.

## How InfluencersNepal works?

1. **Sign Up**
   Click on 'Join as Influencer' and fill out your profile.

2. **Link Your Accounts**
   Authenticate your social media accounts to ensure seamless data integration.

3. **Get Matched**
   Get connected with businesses that align with your niche and goals.

4. **Chat & Collaborate**
   Connect with brands using our built-in chat feature to discuss campaign details.

5. **Track & Report**
   Access detailed analytics of your campaign's performance through your influencer dashboard.

## About Us

We built this platform to solve a disconnect: Nepal's creators drown in self-promotion while businesses burn budgets on mismatched influencers. Our answer? A bridge.

Using smart algorithms, we pair hidden creative talent—introverts, niche experts, even clueless rookies—with brands that genuinely align with their audience, cutting out middlemen and guesswork.

For creators, it's earning sustainably; for businesses, it's authentic campaigns that stick. No fluff, just growth.

Let's fix the broken influencer economy—one meaningful collaboration at a time. 🚀

### Key Pillars
- **Smart Matching**: Connecting the right creators with the perfect brands.
- **Authentic Growth**: Focus on genuine engagement and real results.
- **Sustainable Earnings**: Help creators build lasting income streams.
- **Future Ready**: Evolving with the digital marketing landscape.
